We're excited to roll out Drivebase v3.4.2, packed with updates that make handling your files quicker and more intuitive. Whether you're browsing folders, searching for resources, or tweaking settings, these changes put more power at your fingertips.

## Highlights

- **Right-Click Context Menus**: Right-click on any file or folder to instantly access actions like viewing details, downloading, renaming, favoriting, sharing a link, or deleting. Bulk delete multiple selections at once for faster cleanup.
- **Upgraded Command Palette**: Search for quick links to our GitHub repo, Discord community, bug reporting, or starring the project—right from the palette. No more digging through menus!

## File Explorer Improvements

Your file browsing just got smoother:
- Grid view now displays provider icons and file sizes at a glance, so you know exactly what's what without extra clicks.
- Arrow keys let you navigate the explorer effortlessly—up, down, left, right for speedy movement.
- Subtle scroll fade gradients make long lists easier on the eyes.

## Command Palette and Navigation Boosts

- The command palette now surfaces helpful community links as you type, connecting you to support, updates, and feedback channels in seconds.
- Settings sidebar stays highlighted and active across all settings pages—no more losing your place.

## Other Improvements

- Polished UI touches for a cleaner, more responsive look throughout the app.
- Better onboarding with multilingual support and a handy "Need Help?" button.

## Fixes

- Sidebar navigation now reliably keeps the settings section active on every settings page.

Update to v3.4.2 today and experience the difference. As always, your feedback drives us forward—[join our Discord](https://discord.gg/5hPZwTPp68) or [report ideas](https://github.com/drivebase/drivebase/issues/new)! 🚀
