We're excited to roll out **Drivebase v3.4.0**, packed with features that make syncing, accessing, and organizing your files even smoother. Whether you're automating backups or customizing your workspace, these updates put you in control.

![Highlights illustration](https://cdn.drivebase.io/SCR-20260306-m37.png)

## Highlights

### Powerful Auto-Sync with Custom Schedules
Never worry about manual syncs again. Toggle auto-sync on for your workspace and set your own schedule—daily, weekly, or whatever fits your routine. Choose to sync everything or just selected providers, ensuring your files stay up-to-date effortlessly.

### WebDAV Server for Seamless Access
Connect your favorite tools directly to Drivebase with our new WebDAV server. Generate secure credentials to mount your workspace as a network drive on your desktop or mobile apps. It's perfect for apps that support WebDAV, giving you native-like access to your files.

### New Files Grid View
Switch to a sleek grid layout for browsing your files. See thumbnails, previews, and key details at a glance, making it faster to find and manage what you need.

![Features illustration](https://cdn.drivebase.io/SCR-20260306-m0u.png)

## Improvements

- **Personalization Settings**: Customize your app's look with appearance presets that save automatically. Light, dark, or your own vibe—pick what works best and it sticks.
- **Collapsible Right Panel**: Maximize your screen space by collapsing the side panel when you don't need it. A cleaner view for focused work.
- **Enhanced UI Polish**: Enjoy smoother animations, backdrop blurs, and refined dropdowns for a more modern, responsive experience.
- **Forgot Password Flow**: Recover your account easily with secure OTP verification—no more locked-out headaches.
- **JavaScript SDK**: Power users and teams can now integrate Drivebase more easily into their apps and workflows with our new SDK support.

![Improvements illustration](https://cdn.drivebase.io/SCR-20260306-lzc.png)

## Fixes

We've squashed bugs for better reliability:
- Improved domain resolution and SEO for smoother navigation.
- Better error handling and validation for chunked uploads and syncs.

These updates make Drivebase faster and more dependable every day.
